About Me

My name is Joe Razo,PhD candidate in Clinical Psychology at Palo Alto University, where I study ways to integrate machine learning techniques into health care systems to improve program outcomes. My dissertation (defended July 2021) focused on comparing statistical inference and GLM to statistical learning and associated learning-based algorithms to improve predictive accuracy, using the SAMHSA TEDs dataset. Along with this project, I engage in program evaluation with LifeMoves using quantitative insights is provide insights and make recommendations to decision makers regarding program development and effectiveness. I also engage in some freelance data work, with real estate data where I use deep learning networks to provide market analysis to key stakeholders.

I am a 6-year U.S. Navy veteran, where I spent 4 years stationed in Japan and traveling up and down the western pacific. I enjoy learning about different cultures and trying new foods. In my free time I like to spend time with my family and go on hikes with my dog Nala. During the winter months you can find me on the slopes in Tahoe.
